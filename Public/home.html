About Evo

Being the founders of CrossFit Evo, we have very different backgrounds, come from different areas athletically, and have different goals physically. What we do have in common is a love for CrossFit, people, adventure, and a variety of physical activities. Aside from the added(and maybe most important) benefit of the community we create at our facility, we believe that the more levels and varieties of exercise we involve our members in, the better. We are in full support of any and all of our members working to train for outside accomplishments, such as Spartan Races, competitions, and marathons. And we will be there every step of the way to ensure that you safely and efficiently get you where you need to be, both physically and mentally.
